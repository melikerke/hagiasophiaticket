Istanbul Bosphorus Cruise

★ 4.6 (377)⏱ 1–2 hours✓ Mobile ticket✓ Instant confirmation

About this ticket

Glide along the strait that divides two continents, past Ottoman palaces, fortresses and waterfront mansions. The single most-loved Istanbul experience.

Highlights

  • Cruise between two continents — Europe and Asia at once
  • Pass Dolmabahce and Ciragan palaces, Ortakoy Mosque and Rumeli Fortress
  • See the historic waterfront mansions known as yalıs
  • Glide beneath the Bosphorus Bridge

Local expert tip

Choose sunset over midday — most Istanbul residents would. The light is dramatically better and the city begins to glow.

Good to know before you go

  • Bring a light jacket — evenings feel cool on the water
  • Sit on deck for the best views
  • Sunset departures sell out fastest — book early
