Top Things To See Inside Hagia Sophia

⏱ 5 min read✦ Local expert guideUpdated 2026

If your time is limited, focus on the features that make Hagia Sophia unlike anywhere else in Istanbul: the dome, mosaics, imperial details, Ottoman calligraphy and the Upper Gallery.

Quick answer

The five must-sees are the dome, Deesis Mosaic, Imperial Door, Ottoman calligraphy panels and Upper Gallery.

1. The Dome

The dome is the most famous feature. When Hagia Sophia was completed in 537 AD, it was considered one of the greatest engineering achievements in the world.

2. Deesis Mosaic

The Deesis Mosaic is one of the finest Byzantine mosaics associated with Hagia Sophia. It is a key reason many visitors spend extra time in the gallery areas.

3. Imperial Door

The Imperial Door was historically used by emperors. It is easy to pass quickly, but it helps connect the building to its Byzantine ceremonial past.

4. Ottoman Calligraphy

Massive circular calligraphy panels dominate the interior and are among the largest examples of Islamic calligraphy anywhere in the world.

5. Upper Gallery

The Upper Gallery offers the best overall views inside Hagia Sophia. Many visitors consider this section the highlight of the experience.

Local Tip

Local tip. Many visitors leave after seeing the dome. The upper gallery often contains the most memorable details, so save enough time for it.
