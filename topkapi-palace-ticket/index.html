Quick answer

The featured Topkapi Palace & Harem Section Ticket + Audio Guide starts from €69.95. This option includes hosted entry into the palace, Harem section access and an audio guide. Important difference: host vs guide

The €69.95 option is not a live guided tour. You meet the host, enter the palace together, then explore Topkapi Palace and the Harem at your own pace using the audio guide.

If you want live explanation during the visit, choose the guided tour option from €74.95 on the same booking link.

Starting times

For the sample date Wednesday, July 22, 2026, available hosted-entry times shown include 9:30 AM, 12:00 PM and 2:30 PM. The guided-tour option shows 9:30 AM, 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM. Times can change, so always confirm the selected date on the booking page.

Official gate price vs online option

The official National Palaces visitor page lists the foreign visitor combined ticket for Topkapi Palace + Hagia Irene + Harem as 2750 TL. The online ticket offered here is priced in euros and adds host meeting, timed entry planning, Harem access and audio guide convenience.

Best order with Hagia Sophia

Visit Hagia Sophia early, then continue to Topkapi Palace before lunch or early afternoon. Topkapi takes longer than most Sultanahmet attractions, so give yourself at least 2 to 3 hours if you want to see the palace and Harem without rushing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the ticket include Harem?

Yes. The featured options include the Harem section.

Is this a guided tour?

The €69.95 option is hosted entry plus audio guide, not a live guided tour after entry. The €74.95 option is the guided tour option.

Is Topkapi Palace closed on Tuesday?

The official National Palaces page lists Topkapi Palace as closed on Tuesdays.

Can I combine Topkapi Palace with Hagia Sophia?

Yes. They are close enough for the same day, but start early because Topkapi Palace needs more time.
