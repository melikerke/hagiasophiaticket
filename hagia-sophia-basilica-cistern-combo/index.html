Hagia Sophia Basilica Cistern combo: why it converts well

This is the easiest two-attraction plan in Sultanahmet: Hagia Sophia gives you the dome, mosaics and Ottoman layers; Basilica Cistern gives you the underground Byzantine atmosphere only a short walk away.

What is included

The offer includes the Hagia Sophia visitor ticket, Basilica Cistern entry and audio guidance. The current option does not include Topkapi Palace.

Why combine them

Hagia Sophia delivers monumental scale and religious history; the cistern offers columns, reflections and an underground atmosphere only a short walk away.

Best schedule

Visit Hagia Sophia near opening, take a short break, then enter the cistern later the same morning or on another valid day. Do not schedule both too tightly around Friday prayer.

Who should choose it

It suits first-time visitors who want two major sites without managing separate ticket purchases. Families should allow a break between attractions.
