Topkapi Palace Ticket Price 2026

Official price: 2750 TLOnline from €69.95

Topkapi Palace pricing can be confusing because visitors compare Turkish lira gate prices, euro-based online tickets, Harem access and guided tour options.

Quick answer

The official National Palaces visitor information lists the foreign visitor combined ticket for Topkapi Palace + Hagia Irene + Harem as 2750 TL. The online hosted Topkapi Palace & Harem Section Ticket + Audio Guide featured here starts from €69.95. The guided tour option with Harem ticket and audio guide starts from €74.95.

Why does the online ticket cost more?

The online ticket is not simply the raw gate tariff converted into euros. It includes booking convenience, meeting a host at a fixed time, Harem section access and an audio guide. The guided option adds live guide service.

Does the Harem cost extra?

For these featured options, the Harem section is included. This matters because many travelers specifically want to see the Harem and compare it separately from the general palace grounds.

Which option should you choose?

Choose the €69.95 hosted-entry option if you are comfortable exploring independently with audio commentary. Choose the €74.95 guided tour if this is your first time in Istanbul, if you want historical context, or if you do not want to piece together the palace story alone.

Starting times and planning

Sample starting times shown for Wednesday, July 22, 2026 include 9:30 AM, 12:00 PM and afternoon options. Availability changes by date, so check the booking page before building your itinerary.

Local planning tip

Do not leave Topkapi Palace too late in the day. It is much larger than Basilica Cistern or Blue Mosque, and the Harem adds extra walking and viewing time. A late-morning or early-afternoon slot is usually easier than rushing at the end of the day.
