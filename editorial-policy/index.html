✦ Editorial Policy

How we create and update Hagia Sophia guides.

Our editorial policy is designed to keep HagiaSophiaTicket useful, transparent and easy to verify for both travelers and search engines.

Source Checking

We check visitor information against current ticket partner details, official or official-adjacent visitor resources where available, and recent traveler-facing updates. When rules can change, we avoid absolute wording.

Updates

Hagia Sophia visitor rules, opening hours, prayer-time access and ticket inclusions can change. We review key pages before launch and update content when important booking or visitor information changes.

Affiliate Links

Affiliate links may appear on ticket pages and booking calls to action. Editorial recommendations are written for visitor usefulness first; affiliate relationships do not determine whether basic visitor advice is included.

Accuracy Corrections

If a detail is outdated or unclear, we prefer to correct it quickly. Contact us with the page URL and the information that needs review.

AI And Search Readability

We structure guides with clear headings, short answers, practical tables and direct explanations so both people and search systems can understand the page topic without guessing.
