Hagia Sophia Opening Hours 2026: Best Time To Visit & Prayer Times

⏱ 7 min read✦ Visitor guideUpdated 2026

Planning a visit to Hagia Sophia? Discover current opening hours, prayer times, the best time to visit, how long to spend there, and tips to avoid crowds.

Quick answer

As of June 2026, the upper-gallery tourist route generally opens 08:00-19:00 from April to October and 09:00-19:00 from November to March. It closes to tourists on Fridays from approximately 12:00 to 14:30.

What Are Hagia Sophia Opening Hours?

From April to October, the upper-gallery tourist route generally operates daily from 08:00 to 19:00. From November to March, it generally operates from 09:00 to 19:00. Last admission is normally around 18:30.

These hours were checked for June 2026. Religious events, prayer arrangements and restoration work can cause same-day changes.

Is Hagia Sophia Open Every Day?

The tourist route normally opens seven days a week, including weekends. Friday has a scheduled midday interruption for congregational prayer.

Hagia Sophia is an active mosque, so access is managed differently from a conventional museum.

How Do Prayer Times Affect Visitors?

The paid cultural-visitor route is separated from the ground-floor worship area, but mosque operations can still affect entry. Security screening remains mandatory for every visitor.

Online tickets skip the ticket-office line, not the security line.

Friday Visits

The tourist route closes on Fridays from approximately 12:00 to 14:30 for congregational prayer.

If Friday is your only option, finish before noon or arrive after 15:00 to allow the reopening crowd to clear.

Best Time To Visit

Arrive at the seasonal opening time for the smallest crowds: 08:00 in summer or 09:00 in winter. After 17:00 is the next-best option on ordinary days.

Avoid Friday midday and check for religious-holiday changes before traveling.

Worst Time To Visit

Avoid 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM when possible. This is when tour groups, cruise passengers and midday crowds often overlap.

The same building can feel completely different depending on whether you arrive early or during the peak rush.

How Long Does A Visit Take?

Most visitors spend 45 to 90 minutes on the upper-gallery route. Architecture and history enthusiasts may want longer.

If your ticket includes audio commentary or an AR experience, allow extra time.

Combining Hagia Sophia With Nearby Attractions

Hagia Sophia pairs easily with the Blue Mosque, Basilica Cistern, Topkapi Palace and Sultanahmet Square.

Place Hagia Sophia first if it is your top priority, then build the rest of the day around crowd levels and walking distance.

Sources And Update Note

This guide was updated in June 2026 using the official ticket tariff and current visitor guidance. Religious events and restoration work can cause same-day changes.

Local Tips From Istanbul

  • Visit early in the morning if this topic matters to your experience.
  • Look up often; many of Hagia Sophia's most important details are above eye level.
  • Use the Upper Gallery for better context, photos and architectural perspective.
  • Book with enough time around prayer periods and Sultanahmet crowds.
