Basilica Cistern Ticket Price 2026

Official: 1,950 TL daytimeGuided: from €57.47

The Basilica Cistern has two useful price numbers to understand: the official entry tariff and the price of guided fast-track tickets sold by tour partners.

Quick answer

The official daytime foreign visitor ticket is listed at 1,950 TL. The Night Shift foreign visitor ticket is listed at 3,000 TL. The English guided fast-track ticket featured here starts from €57.47.

Is the guided ticket the cheapest option?

No. If the lowest possible entry cost is your only goal, the official daytime ticket is the benchmark. The guided fast-track ticket costs more because it adds an English guide and a smoother visit structure.

Who should book the guided ticket?

  • First-time visitors who want context, not just photos.
  • Travelers combining Hagia Sophia and Basilica Cistern in one day.
  • Visitors who prefer a guided explanation of the Medusa heads and Byzantine water system.
  • People who value convenience over the absolute lowest ticket price.

Museum Pass and free entry

The Basilica Cistern is not affiliated with the Ministry of Culture, so Museum Pass Istanbul is not the right framework for this attraction. The official visitor page states free entry for Turkish citizens aged 65 and over and for visitors under 7.
