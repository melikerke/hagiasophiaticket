This guided tour is one of the cleanest ways to see Sultanahmet's three most practical landmarks in one route: Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque and the Basilica Cistern.

Quick answer

Choose this tour if you want a guided English route, meeting in Sultanahmet Old Town, with skip-the-line tickets included where stated. The shared group option starts from €107.10.

Why this route works

Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque face the same historic square, while Basilica Cistern is only a short walk away. A guided route removes the guesswork around order, timing and what to notice inside each place.
