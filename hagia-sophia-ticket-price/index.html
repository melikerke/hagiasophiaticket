Ticket price

Hagia Sophia Ticket Price 2026

6 min readUpdated June 2026

The standard on-site ticket for the upper-gallery tourist route costs €25 as of June 2026.

Quick answer

Pay €25 at the on-site ticket office, or book online from €28 for advance confirmation, audio commentary and AR features. Children under 8 enter free with ID.

Current ticket prices

The official foreign-visitor tariff is €25 for the upper-gallery route. The online offer on this site begins at €28 and includes digital services. Prices shown by ticket partners can change.

Why the online ticket costs more

The difference covers advance booking, mobile confirmation, audio commentary and augmented-reality features. It also avoids the ticket-office queue, but never the mandatory security screening.

Children and passes

Children under 8 enter free when age is proven with a passport or official ID. Museum Pass Istanbul does not include the standard foreign-visitor ticket; Türkiye-issued MüzeKart rules are separate.

Which option should you choose?

Choose the €25 on-site ticket if price is your only priority and you accept the ticket-office queue. Choose the online option when planning certainty and the digital guide matter more.
