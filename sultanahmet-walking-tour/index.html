Sultanahmet Walking Tour: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque & Old City

From €54.28English guide2.5 hours+

A Sultanahmet walking tour is the easiest choice if you do not want to plan every entrance, prayer-time pause and walking route yourself. It keeps Hagia Sophia at the center but adds context around the Blue Mosque, the Old City and, depending on the option, Basilica Cistern.

Quick answer

The best low-commitment option is the shared group tour with Hagia Sophia included from €54.28. Choose the higher-priced small-group Sultanahmet Old Town option from €122.55 if you want a more guided, premium Old City experience.

Who should book this instead of only a ticket?

Book a walking tour if you want the story of Sultanahmet explained while you move between landmarks. Book only a ticket if you prefer to enter Hagia Sophia independently and spend your budget on additional attractions such as Basilica Cistern or Topkapi Palace.

Local planning tip

If this is your first morning in Istanbul, choose the 9:00 AM start when available. Sultanahmet becomes busier later in the day, and morning light is usually better for photos around Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hagia Sophia included?

Yes, the shared group option listed here includes Hagia Sophia. Always confirm the selected option on the booking page before payment.

Is this better than a combo ticket?

It is better if you want a guide. A combo ticket is better if you mainly want pre-arranged entrance tickets and flexible timing.

Where does the tour meet?

The small-group option meets in Sultanahmet Old Town. Exact meeting details are shown on the booking page.
