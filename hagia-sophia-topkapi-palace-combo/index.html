Hagia Sophia + Topkapi Palace Combo Ticket

6 min readUpdated June 2026

Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace together explain the religious and imperial history of Istanbul's historic peninsula.

Quick answer

The complete three-attraction offer starts from €147 and adds Basilica Cistern. Topkapi uses a hosted timed entry; Hagia Sophia and the cistern are flexible within the stated validity.

What the complete combo includes

Tickets cover Hagia Sophia's visitor area, Basilica Cistern and Topkapi Palace, plus online audio guides. Harem entry is not included.

Topkapi timed entry

Topkapi Palace requires meeting the host for one of the available entry slots. Arrive early; missing the host can affect palace access.

Best order

Start with Hagia Sophia near opening, continue to Topkapi for the booked slot, and use Basilica Cistern later or on another valid day.

Who should choose it

Choose this option for a full history-focused itinerary. It is less suitable for travelers who prefer a slow half-day or families with very young children.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the complete combo?

Currently from €147.

Is the Harem included?

No.

Is Topkapi flexible?

No, it uses hosted timed entry.

Can the other tickets be used separately?

Yes, according to the offer's stated validity.
