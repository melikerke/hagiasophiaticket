Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information may be processed when you use HagiaSophiaTicket.

Who controls the site

HagiaSophiaTicket is operated by its editorial owner, Melike. Privacy questions can be sent to hello@hagiasophiaticket.com.

Analytics and consent

Google Analytics 4 is configured through Google Consent Mode v2. Analytics storage is denied by default until you accept analytics cookies. When enabled, GA4 may process page views, approximate location, device information and interactions such as affiliate clicks.

Affiliate links and bookings

We record which ticket offer was clicked, its displayed price, page language and button placement. Payments and booking details are handled by the external ticket partner; HagiaSophiaTicket does not receive card details.

Storage and third parties

Your consent choice is stored locally in your browser. GitHub Pages and linked services may process technical server logs under their own policies. Retention for GA4 data follows our Analytics property settings.

Your choices

You can reject analytics cookies without losing access to the site. Reopen Cookie Settings from the Cookie Policy page. For access, correction or deletion questions, email us.
